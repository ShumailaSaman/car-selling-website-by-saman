FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell my car on this website?

You can list your car by creating an account, clicking on the "Sell Your Car" button, and filling out the details about your vehicle, including its make, model, year, condition, and price.

Is there a fee to list my car for sale?

Listing your car on our website is free of charge. However, we may offer premium options for highlighted listings or priority placement for a small fee.

How can I find the right car for me?

Use our search filters to narrow down the options based on your preferences, such as make, model, price range, year, mileage, and location.

Are the prices negotiable?

Prices are set by the sellers and may be negotiable. You can contact the seller directly to discuss the price or make an offer.

How do I contact the seller?

You can contact the seller by clicking the "Contact Seller" button on the car listing page, which will allow you to send them a message or view their contact details.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payment methods vary depending on the seller. It's recommended to discuss the payment options with the seller directly before making any transaction.

Is there any warranty on the cars listed?

Warranty information is provided by the individual sellers. Some cars may come with a warranty, while others are sold as-is. Make sure to clarify this with the seller before purchasing.

How do I report a suspicious listing?

If you encounter a suspicious listing, please click on the "Report" button available on the listing page or contact our support team with the details of the issue.

Can I edit or delete my listing?

Yes, you can edit or delete your listing by logging into your account, going to your dashboard, and selecting the listing you wish to modify or remove.
